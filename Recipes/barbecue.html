Barbecue Ribs Recipes

Description

This is a true southern charm recipe. Use for your next grill out.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs pork spareribs
  • 1 c. brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. chile sauce
  • 1/4 c. soy sauce
  • 1/4 c. worchestershire sauce
  • 1/4 c. rum
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1 dash black pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Cut spareribs into serving size portions; wrap in double thickness of foil.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 Â½ hours. Unwrap and drain drippings. (I usually freeze drippings to use later in soups.) Place ribs in a large roasting pan.

  4. Step 4

    5. Mix brown sugar, chile sauce, ketchup, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, rum, garlic, mustard, and pepper together in a bowl. Coat ribs with sauce and marinate at room temperature for 1 hour or refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.

  5. Step 5

    6. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Position the grate 4 inches above heat source; grease the grate with cooking spray.

  6. Step 6

    7. Cook ribs on the preheated grill for 30 minutes, basting with marinade.
