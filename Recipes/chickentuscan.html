Chicken Tuscan Recipe

Description

This is a creamy delicious poultry meal sure to make your family satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 c. heavy cream
  • 1/2 c. chicken broth
  • 1/2 c. grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 ts italian seasoning
  • 1 c. spinach chopped
  • 1/2 c. sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until browned and no longer pink in the center, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Remove chicken and set aside on a plate.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add heavy cream, chicken broth, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Whisk sauce over medium-high heat until starting to thicken, about 5 minutes. Add spinach and sun-dried tomatoes; simmer until spinach starts to wilt, about 1 minute. Return chicken to the skillet and cook until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
